# ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀CATWORLD RP

## What is this server?

**CatWorld RP** — a roleplay server for cat lovers and RP enthusiasts. Commands like /me and /do are supported, plus cars and houses! The map and city are fictional and still under construction.

## Mods and Resource Packs

- **CatWorld Resourcepack** — a custom lightweight resource pack that changes the GUI interface.  
- **Other Mods** — client and server mods downloaded from the internet. Required to join. Version: **1.21.1 Fabric**.

## When is the launch?

**COMING SOON!** — the exact date is unknown. More coding and promotion are needed before release.

## Economy

- **Money** — the server’s currency, given to every player at the start. Spend it on needs or desires.  
- **Real Estate** — houses and apartments will be affordable, priced at the minimum.  
- **Cars** — still uncertain, since Fabric doesn’t have ready‑made car mods. Custom models may be created in Blockbench.
